Top 5 critical findings:
1. **Three clone schemas (`b2b`, `b2b_ecosystem`, `jsblueridge`) have 100% table-name overlap (32 tables each).** Row counts diverge: `b2b` is the populated ecommerce pipeline, `b2b_ecosystem` is completely empty (2 rows total), `jsblueridge` holds 17k orders. 2. **Identity ambiguity: `employee_id` FKs to both `hq.employees` AND `hrms.employees` AND `public.ai_employees` across different tables** â€” `public.users.employee_id` is text with no FK, `public.employee_skills.employee_id` points to `public.ai_employees`, all others go to `hrms.employees` or `hq.employees`. 4. **295 foreign-key columns lack a covering index.** Worst offenders are large tables: `public.calls.user_phone_id` (5,361 rows), `ets.pos_sale_items.product_id`+`sale_id` (5,367 rows), `usdrop.products.supplier_id`+`category_id` (21,005 rows), `ets.products.vendor_id`+`category_l3_id` (22,984 rows). 5. **374 `multiple_permissive_policies` advisor warnings** (mostly `life`, `public`, `b2b`) are shredding query plans; 61 `auth_rls_initplan` warnings ask for `(select auth.uid())` wrapping.
